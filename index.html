The Experience

Discover the Rich Taste of Japanese Green Tea

Embark on a captivating journey into the world of Japanese tea, where a myriad of exquisite flavors awaits your exploration. From the vibrant Sencha green tea to the roasted elegance of Houjicha, and the unique charm of Genmaicha and Matcha green tea powder.

With over 50 distinct tea-producing regions across Japan, the diversity of Japanese tea is boundless. Guided by a tea expert, you'll savor a diverse selection of teas sourced from various farms and witness the art of brewing these leaves to perfection.

* This is not a traditional tea ceremony experience.

Our Collection

Sencha Green Tea

Japan's most beloved tea

The most produced type of green tea in Japan. We explore the difference in grade and taste across the regions — from everyday cups to superior grade leaves.

— 02

Matcha

The world-renowned tea powder

Probably the most well-known Japanese tea around the world. We compare the taste among major regions, served alongside traditional Japanese confectionary.

— 03

Gyokuro

Japan's prestige tea

Looks just like sencha but made by shading the farm. Intense umami and natural sweetness — we taste the superior grade, hand-picked gyokuro.

Your Host

A Decade of Tea Expertise

I am the founder of ShiZen Tea, a small Japanese tea company nestled in downtown Tokyo. My journey into the world of tea began in my childhood, where I had the privilege of witnessing the art of Japanese tea ceremony, thanks to my mother's passion for Sadō.

Over the past decade, I've dedicated myself to the study of Japanese tea, embarking on countless journeys to tea farms and factories across Japan. This pursuit culminated in my certification as a Japanese Tea Adviser.
